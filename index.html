About Us

JIPS

JAIN INTERNATIONAL PUBLIC SCHOOL was established in 2013 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Urban area. It is located in VALSAD block of VALSAD district of Gujrat. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 5. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

The school has a private building. It has 6 classrooms for instructional purposes, all in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for the Headmaster/Teacher. The school has a pucca boundary wall and an electric connection. The source of drinking water is tap water, which is functional. The school has 1 boys' toilet and 1 girls' toilet, both functional. It has a playground, a library with 200 books, and 4 functional computers for teaching and learning purposes. The school does not have a computer-aided learning lab. It does not need ramps for disabled children to access classrooms.
